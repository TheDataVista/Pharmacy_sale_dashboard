
## 💊 Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Dashboard

### 📌 Project Overview

Developed an interactive Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Dashboard as part of the **Onyx Data DNA Challenge**, leveraging **Power BI** and **ZoomCharts** to analyze pharmaceutical retail performance across Europe.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of revenue, profitability, sales growth, product performance, and regional trends, enabling data-driven decision-making for stakeholders.

---

### 📊 Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------|
| Total Revenue | €8.63M |
| Profit Margin | 28% |
| MoM Revenue Growth | 4.6% |
| Units Sold Growth | 5% |

---

### 🔍 Key Business Insights

- Germany and France generated the highest revenue across all regions.
- Urban pharmacies consistently outperformed rural locations.
- OTC products contributed significantly to sales volume.
- Non-promotional products delivered stronger profit margins.
- Regional and product-level analysis revealed opportunities for revenue optimization.
